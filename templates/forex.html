🦅 EUR/JPY → SPY CORRELATION SIGNAL London 3AM EST sets the mood · NY Open 9:30AM execute
🧠 THE WOLF EUR/JPY → SPY STRATEGY:
JPY = safe haven. When investors are bold/greedy → they sell JPY and buy risk assets. EUR/JPY RISES → SPY RISES.
When investors are scared → they dump stocks and run to JPY safety. EUR/JPY DROPS → SPY DROPS.
EUR/JPY is the LEADING indicator — it moves FIRST, then SPY follows 5–15 min later. That lag is your entry window.
⚡ THE 3 TRADE SCENARIOS — KNOW WHICH ONE YOU'RE IN
✅ CONFIRMATION
EUR/JPY breaking new daily highs AND SPY moving up together
→ BUY SPY CALLS
Both moving in sync = strong signal
🚨 DIVERGENCE (TRAP)
SPY trying to rally BUT EUR/JPY is flat or dropping
→ BUY SPY PUTS
Stock rally is FAKE — no risk-on fuel
⚡ THE LEAD
EUR/JPY drops hard in London/pre-market BEFORE US opens
→ BUY PUTS AT 9:30AM
The move started in Europe first

🚨 SIGNAL KILLERS — CHECK BEFORE TRADING
BOJ Announcement — If Bank of Japan hints at rate hikes, JPY spikes suddenly. EUR/JPY correlation breaks temporarily. Skip trading that day.
FOMC / Fed Speaker — USD-centric event. Causes massive risk-off JPY flows. Signal usually valid but expect volatility spikes.
NFP at 8:30AM EST — Jobs report overrides everything. EUR/JPY will whipsaw violently. Wait until 9:45AM to confirm direction.
CPI Data — Inflation data = massive risk-off JPY flows if bad. Trades after CPI are valid if EUR/JPY stabilizes.
ECB Meeting Day — EUR side gets distorted. Use USD/JPY as backup risk indicator instead.
