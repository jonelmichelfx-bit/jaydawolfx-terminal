Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Cancellations apply at end of current billing cycle. No hidden fees.

On Wolf Elite — yes. Real daily, weekly and hourly candles are fetched, actual EMA/RSI/MACD calculated, and real swing high/low S/R levels identified before Claude AI gives any analysis. Wolf Pro uses live prices + news. Both are far more accurate than generic AI signals.
